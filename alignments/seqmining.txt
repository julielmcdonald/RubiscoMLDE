Prompts used to get sequences from NCBI:

Bacteria: cbbM[Gene Name] OR cbbL[Gene Name] OR rbcL[Gene Name] AND eubacteria[Organism] 
Plants: rbcL[Gene Name] AND Tracheophyta[Organism]
Green algae: cbbM[Gene Name] OR cbbL[Gene Name] OR rbcL[Gene Name] AND Chlorophyta[Organism] 
Red algae: cbbM[Gene Name] OR cbbL[Gene Name] OR rbcL[Gene Name] AND rhodophyta[Organism]
Diatoms: (cbbM[Gene Name] OR cbbL[Gene Name] OR rbcL[Gene Name]) AND Bacillariophyta[Organism]
