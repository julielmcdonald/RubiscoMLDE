Prompts used to get sequences from NCBI. Sequences are used for calculations in Supplementary Table 4 and Supplementary Figure 7.

Bacteria: 
cbbM[Gene Name] OR cbbL[Gene Name] OR rbcL[Gene Name] AND eubacteria[Organism] 

Plants: 
rbcL[Gene Name] AND Tracheophyta[Organism]

Green algae: 
cbbM[Gene Name] OR cbbL[Gene Name] OR rbcL[Gene Name] AND Chlorophyta[Organism] 

Red algae: 
cbbM[Gene Name] OR cbbL[Gene Name] OR rbcL[Gene Name] AND rhodophyta[Organism]

Diatoms: 
(cbbM[Gene Name] OR cbbL[Gene Name] OR rbcL[Gene Name]) AND Bacillariophyta[Organism]

mafft --auto /plant_rbcL.fasta > /plant_rbcL_aligned.fasta
